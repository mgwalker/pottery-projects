Glazed

  • dog food bowl

    A food bowl for my dog. Pretty simple hand-build with a paw print scraped into the center of the bowl. For glazing, I used "very white" for the inside and a black to fill in paw print. On the outside, I used "reitz blue" as a base, with streaks of "very white" (white), shaner red (hopefully turns brown at firing), and "iron saturate" (hopefully fires black) to maybe look like dog hair.
